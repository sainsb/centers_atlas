Regional Centers Overview

The 2040 Growth Concept Plan identifies eight Regional Centers that serve different market areas of the region. Regional centers are the focus for compact development, redevelopment and multi-modal transit connections. The 2040 Plan calls for rail connections to tie all the regional centers to each other and to the Central City. Currently six of the eight regional centers are connected by rail. Urban form varies greatly from center to center with some having a grid street pattern and a historic downtown feel and others typified by larger block sizes and large format retail more typical of suburban malls.
